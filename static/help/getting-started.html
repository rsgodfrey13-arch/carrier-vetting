Getting Started

Fast setup: create an account, add carriers, send your first agreement, and enable alerts.

3–6 minutes Beginner

Creating an Account

Getting started takes only a minute. Create your Carrier Shark account, verify your email, and select the plan that fits your operation. Once completed, you can begin monitoring carriers and managing agreements immediately.

Step 1 — Open Create Account
  • Click Create Account from the homepage.
  • Enter your name, work email, company name, and password.
  • Select Create Account to continue.
Step 2 — Verify your email
  • Check your inbox for the Carrier Shark verification email.
  • Click the verification link to confirm your account.
  • After verification, you'll be returned to the platform to continue setup.
Step 3 — Select a plan
  • Choose the plan that fits your monitoring needs.
  • You can begin with Starter and upgrade later as your carrier list grows.
  • Once a plan is selected, you’re ready to begin adding carriers.
Common issue
Didn’t receive the verification email? Check your spam folder or use the Resend Email option on the verification screen.

Adding Your First Carrier

Carrier Shark provides multiple ways to add carriers to your monitoring list. You can add carriers individually, add several carriers at once directly from search results, or import carriers using the Bulk Import Wizard.

Search for a carrier
  • Search by DOT number, MC number, or company name.
  • Open the carrier profile to review authority, safety history, and insurance.
  • Select Add to My Carriers to begin monitoring.
Add carriers in bulk from search
  • Select multiple carriers directly from the search results.
  • Add them to your monitoring list in one action.
  • This is the fastest way to build your watchlist when reviewing multiple carriers.
Import carriers with the Bulk Import Wizard
  • Use the Bulk Import Wizard to upload a list of carriers.
  • Paste or upload DOT numbers to add them instantly.
  • Carrier Shark automatically begins monitoring each imported carrier.

Sending Your First Agreement

Carrier Shark allows you to send broker–carrier agreements for secure electronic signature and collect documents directly from the carrier. Once signed, agreements and related documents are stored inside the carrier profile for easy access.

Step 1 — Upload your agreement
  • Upload your broker–carrier agreement to My Contracts.
  • Supported formats include standard document formats such as PDF.
  • The agreement becomes available to send to carriers for signature.
Step 2 — Send for signature
  • Select a carrier and send the agreement for electronic signature.
  • The carrier receives an email with a secure signing link.
  • The carrier can review and sign the agreement without creating an account.
Step 3 — Access signed agreements and documents
  • Signed agreements appear inside the carrier profile.
  • Carrier documents such as W-9s, insurance certificates, and ACH forms are stored in the documents section.
  • A signing certificate is included for verification and record keeping.

Setting Up Alerts

Carrier Shark continuously monitors your carriers for operational and compliance changes. Alerts can be customized to track the specific carrier events that matter most to your operation.

Step 1 — Choose what to monitor
  • Select the carrier fields that should trigger alerts.
  • Examples include authority changes, safety updates, insurance expirations, and compliance changes.
  • Customize monitoring to match your workflow and risk tolerance.
Step 2 — Choose delivery method
  • Receive alerts instantly by Email.
  • Integrate alerts into your systems using the REST API.
  • Use Webhooks to push alerts into internal workflows automatically.
