Billing & Plans

Compare plans, upgrade your account, and manage billing securely through Stripe.

3–5 minutes Beginner

Plan Comparison

Carrier Shark plans are designed to scale with your operation. You can start small and upgrade as your carrier monitoring needs grow, whether you need a simple watchlist or a fully integrated operational workflow.

Step 1 — Review available plans
  • Compare Starter, Core, Pro, and Enterprise plans from the pricing page.
  • Plan differences include monitored carrier limits, alerts, team access, API availability, webhooks, and document features.
  • Choose the plan that matches your current workflow and expected volume.
Step 2 — Choose a plan that fits your operation
  • Starter is best for exploring the platform and monitoring a small number of carriers.
  • Core and Pro are designed for active broker operations that need more monitoring capacity and alerting flexibility.
  • Enterprise is intended for high-volume teams that need scale, onboarding support, and broader operational coverage.
Tip
Start with the plan that fits your current workflow. You can upgrade later as your carrier volume, team size, or integration needs increase.

Upgrading Your Plan

You can upgrade your plan from your Carrier Shark account at any time. This makes it easy to expand monitoring capacity or unlock additional features as your operation grows.

Step 1 — Open your plan settings
  • Go to My Account and open the Plan section.
  • Use the plan page to review your current subscription and available upgrade options.
  • This is the main place to manage your Carrier Shark plan.
Step 2 — Select Manage Plan
  • Click Manage Plan to continue to billing management.
  • From there, you can upgrade to a higher plan when you need more capacity or features.
  • Plan changes are designed to be fast and straightforward.
Step 3 — Confirm your new plan
  • Choose the new plan that fits your needs and complete checkout.
  • Once confirmed, your updated subscription becomes active for your account.
  • Your new plan determines available limits and billing going forward.

Billing & Payments

Carrier Shark uses Stripe for secure payment processing and subscription billing. This gives you a familiar checkout experience and lets you manage billing through a trusted payment platform.

Step 1 — Complete checkout securely
  • When you upgrade or activate a paid plan, checkout is handled securely through Stripe.
  • This allows you to enter payment information in a familiar, trusted billing flow.
  • Carrier Shark does not store your card details directly.
Step 2 — Understand how billing is managed
  • Billing is managed through Stripe once your subscription is active.
  • You can use the billing portal to review your subscription and payment details.
  • This keeps payment management secure and separate from daily operational work inside Carrier Shark.
Security
Payments are securely processed by Stripe. Carrier Shark does not store full card details on its own servers.

Managing Your Subscription

You can manage your subscription at any time. This includes updating billing details, changing plans, and canceling when needed.

Step 1 — Open billing management
  • Return to the Billing section in My Account.
  • Use the billing or management controls to access your subscription settings.
  • This is where you manage payment method, subscription status, and plan changes.
Step 2 — Update, downgrade, or cancel
  • You can update payment information, change your plan, or cancel your subscription from the billing portal.
  • Subscription management is designed to be self-serve and straightforward.
  • If you need help with billing or account questions, contact support from the Help Center.
Step 3 — Know when to contact Carrier Shark
  • For Enterprise planning, custom limits, or operational onboarding, contact the Carrier Shark team.
  • For technical feature needs such as API or webhook planning, use the appropriate technical support or integrations channel.
  • For standard subscription changes, Stripe billing tools should handle most needs directly.
