Core Product

What is Carrier Shark?

Carrier Shark is a carrier management platform built for brokers and 3PLs who want visibility and control over their carrier network.

Access real-time FMCSA data, manage agreements, collect insurance, and monitor status changes — all from one centralized system.

  • Search carriers by DOT, MC, Legal Name, or DBA
  • Build and manage your private My Carriers database
  • Send your broker-carrier agreement for electronic signature
  • Collect insurance and store details inside the carrier profile
  • Monitor authority and safety changes via Email, API, or Webhook
  • Maintain a complete carrier history in one place
Is Carrier Shark a broker or carrier?

No. Carrier Shark is not a broker, carrier, or factoring company. We’re a software platform that facilitates carrier data management and document workflows.

Where does your carrier data come from?

Carrier data is sourced from FMCSA public records and updated regularly to reflect current authority, safety, and registration status.

How often is FMCSA data updated?

Carrier authority and status information is refreshed continuously as updates become available from FMCSA records.

Can I upload my own broker-carrier agreement?

Yes. You can upload your own agreement and send it directly to carriers for electronic signature. Signed documents are stored securely in the carrier’s profile.

Do you provide a broker-carrier agreement template?

Carrier Shark may provide sample templates for convenience, but we do not provide legal advice. You’re responsible for ensuring your agreement meets your business and legal requirements.

Insurance & Monitoring

How does insurance collection work?

When you send your agreement, carriers can upload their certificate of insurance. Carrier Shark extracts and stores insurance details inside the carrier’s profile.

Do you verify insurance?

Carrier Shark collects and organizes insurance documentation. Final verification and approval decisions remain the responsibility of the broker.

What alerts can I receive?

You can receive alerts for:

  • Authority status changes
  • Out-of-service updates
  • Insurance expirations
  • Safety rating changes
  • Contract updates

Alerts can be delivered via:

  • Email
  • REST API
  • Webhook

API & Integrations

Do you offer an API?

Yes. Pro and Enterprise plans include API access for direct system integration.

Can I connect Carrier Shark to my TMS?

Yes. Carrier Shark can integrate via API or webhook into most modern TMS platforms.

Do you offer webhooks?

Yes. Real-time webhook notifications are available on supported plans.

Carrier Management

What is “My Carriers”?

“My Carriers” is your private database of carriers you track, contract with, or monitor.

Can I bulk upload carriers?

Yes. You can bulk add carriers via upload or API.

What happens when a carrier’s authority changes?

You’ll receive alerts based on your configured notification settings.

Do carriers need to create an account?

No. Carriers do not need to create an account to sign agreements or upload documents.
