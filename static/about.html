Built for Broker Control

Carrier Shark exists to bring structure, visibility, and operational control to carrier relationships inside modern freight brokerages.

Why Carrier Shark Exists

Freight brokers operate in an environment where carrier authority status, insurance validity, documentation, and agreements directly impact risk exposure. Yet most workflows rely on scattered spreadsheets, inbox searches, or manual tracking.

Carrier Shark centralizes carrier visibility into a single system designed specifically for brokers — not compliance checklists, not onboarding gimmicks, and not generic document storage.

What We Focus On

  • Carrier authority and safety visibility
  • Insurance document tracking
  • Broker-carrier agreement management
  • Real-time monitoring for status changes
  • Operational accountability

Our Approach

Carrier Shark is designed as infrastructure — not a marketing layer. Every feature is built to reduce ambiguity and increase operational clarity.

We prioritize system integrity, structured workflows, and broker-first control. The goal is not to replace judgment — but to support it with better visibility.

Built for Modern Brokerages

As freight operations become more digitized, the need for structured carrier oversight becomes more critical. Carrier Shark is designed to integrate into that environment — cleanly and intentionally.
