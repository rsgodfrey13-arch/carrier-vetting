What you can do

  • Search by DOT, MC, legal name, DBA, or city + state.
  • Pull carrier details including operating status, safety rating, and insurance summary.
  • Track carriers in your account and retrieve alerts.
  • Send and monitor carrier contracts.
  • Import carriers in bulk with plan-aware limits.

Example request

curl --request GET \
  --url "https://carriershark.com/api/v1/carriers?dot=336075" \
  --header "Authorization: Bearer cs_live_your_api_key" \
  --header "Accept: application/json"

Endpoint preview

Focused API surface for carrier search, monitoring, and onboarding flows.

GET /carriers

Search by DOT, MC, legal name, DBA, or city + state.

GET /carriers/{dot}

Retrieve a single carrier record by DOT number.

GET /me/carriers

List saved carriers for your company account.

GET /alerts/new

Fetch newly generated alerts for tracked carriers.

POST /me/carriers/import

Bulk import DOTs into saved carriers with limits enforced.

POST /contracts/send/{dot}

Send a carrier contract inside your onboarding workflow.
