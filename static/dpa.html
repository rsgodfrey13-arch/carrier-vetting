1. Scope and Relationship

This Data Processing Addendum (“DPA”) forms part of the Carrier Shark Terms of Service and any applicable Order Form (collectively, the “Agreement”) between [Carrier Shark Legal Name, LLC] (“Processor”) and the customer entity (“Controller”).

To the extent Carrier Shark processes Personal Information on behalf of Controller, Carrier Shark acts as a data processor/service provider and Controller acts as the data controller/business.

Carrier Shark does not determine the purposes for which customer documents are created, uploaded, or transmitted.

2. Nature of Processing

Carrier Shark processes Personal Information solely to provide the Platform, including document transmission, storage, workflow execution, OCR processing, electronic signature facilitation, and security monitoring.

Categories of Data Subjects may include:

  • Broker personnel
  • Carrier personnel
  • Shipper personnel
  • Authorized representatives signing agreements

Categories of Personal Information may include:

  • Name
  • Business contact information (email, phone)
  • Signature metadata (timestamp, IP address, audit trail)
  • Information contained in uploaded business documents

3. Processor Obligations

Carrier Shark agrees to:

  • Process Personal Information only on documented instructions from Controller.
  • Not sell or retain Personal Information outside the business purpose of providing the Platform.
  • Ensure personnel are subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards.

4. Subprocessors

Controller authorizes Carrier Shark to use subprocessors to provide the Platform. Subprocessors may include infrastructure, hosting, storage, monitoring, and OCR providers.

Current subprocessors may include:

  • DigitalOcean (cloud hosting and infrastructure)
  • DigitalOcean Spaces (document storage)
  • DocuPipe (OCR processing)
  • Sentry (monitoring and error tracking)
  • GitHub (code repository management)

Carrier Shark will require subprocessors to implement appropriate safeguards consistent with this DPA.

5. Security Measures

Carrier Shark maintains reasonable security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit
  • Access controls and least-privilege practices
  • Email-based verification codes for certain signing workflows
  • Monitoring and logging for platform integrity

6. Data Subject Requests

To the extent legally required, Carrier Shark will provide reasonable assistance to Controller in responding to verified data subject access, correction, or deletion requests.

7. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a confirmed security incident affecting Personal Information, Carrier Shark will notify Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of the incident.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Upon termination of the Agreement and written request from Controller, Carrier Shark will delete or return Personal Information, except where retention is required by law or necessary for legitimate business purposes such as dispute resolution.

9. Cross-Border Transfers

Personal Information may be processed in the United States or other jurisdictions where Carrier Shark or its subprocessors operate. Carrier Shark will implement reasonable safeguards consistent with applicable law.

10. Limitation of Liability

This DPA is subject to the limitations of liability set forth in the Carrier Shark Terms of Service and any applicable Order Form.
